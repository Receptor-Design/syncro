 Introducing the "Tabs Block" plugin for WordPress – your key to flexibility and creativity! This versatile block lets you add any blocks inside for content and provides unique features like separate tabs buttons and tab content blocks, empowering you to design layouts just the way you want. Plus, with the Collapsible block, you can hide content for inactive tabs buttons, revealing it only when the corresponding tab is active. Enjoy the freedom of great styling options to make your tabs stand out effortlessly. Unleash your content presentation like never before!
